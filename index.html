Chocolate Pizza

For the fig-swirl: Melt butter over medium heat in a saucepan. Add brown sugar and stir to dissolve. Halve all of the figs and toss in the saucepan with water and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until you have a chunky-jammy mixture. Add salt with on or two sirs, set aside and let cool completey.

Ice crea; In a small pot over medium heat, combine milk, and granulated sugar until sugar is completely dissolved and the milk is just barely lukewarm. Whisk in the egg yolkd. Set mixture in the fridge and wait until the fig mixture is cooled.

Using an ice cream machine, pour liquids into the frozen basein and process according to manufacturer instructions, i.e., let spin and thicken for 20 minutes before add mascarpone, fig jam mixture, and the nuts. Continue to process for +/- 10 minutes. Pour semi-frozen mixture in to a pyrex dish or glass tupperware. Freeze for at leat two hours before serving.

  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup mascarpone
  • 1/2 tsp pink salt
  • 1 lb Black Mission Figs
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2-4 tbsp water
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/3 ganulated sugar
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup honey roasted pecans, roughly chopped
